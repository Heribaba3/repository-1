Propreitress Speech
De-Glory Academy is a co-educational primary and secondary school located at 6, babatunde odusiji close, Ipamesan, Sango Ota Ogun State, Nigeria. It is a full boarding school with well-qualified and experienced teaching staff who stand in “loco parentis” to the pupils.

The school opened in September, 2005. When fully grown, the total number of pupils in both Primary and Secondary Schools shall be about 200.

The location of the school is serene and conducive to learning. When fully developed the facilities provided on the master plan are sure to further enrich the pupils’ school experience.

It is important, that pupils coming into the school must have acquired the basic skills of personal hygiene, washing their own clothes and keeping their surrounding clean. It is therefore mandatory that all applicants must be at least 10 years old.

The school is a unifying factor for the high and the lowly. Apart from the academic curriculum, a wide range of co-curricular activities are put in place to encourage development of the pupils’ natural talents and creative abilities.

At the end of a 6-year sojourn in the school, a total child would have evolved, fully groomed to stand out tall among his/her peers in academics, commitment to duty, high moral values and service to the Nation and mankind.

CEO of DE-GLORY ACADEMY
