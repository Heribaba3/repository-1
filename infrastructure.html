Infrastructure

 The school is located at 6, babatunde odusiji close, Ipamesan, Sango ota, Ogun State,Nigeria. The school architectural master plan made provision for libraries classroom blocks, laboratories, library,School hall, school farm, sporting facilities, hostels and accommodation for principal, staff and pupils. Indeed,concerted effort are being made under the Administration headed by Pastor Olufemi Ogundare, who is the school advicer and implemented by Mrs Mercy Ogundare to further develop the facilities based on the details of the highly comprehensive master plan.

There are standard classrooms to accommodate pupils in each class.

The school has well-equipped laboratories for Integrated Agricultural sciences, Physics, Chemistry and Biology.

The school provides a computer room with latest of computers linked with the internet for staff and students to browse and be connected to the rest of the world.

There is standard school library with seating capacity of nearly 60 students at a time and it conforms to laid down standard for school.

The school provides special room/centers for music, computer studies, technical drawing, fine art and Home Economics which also caters for the teaching of food and nutrition, home management, clothing and textile etc. There are also special rooms for typewriting and art/ crafts.

There is enough open space for sports and outdoor games, coupled with adequate provision of regular water supply and toilet facilities.

The school has a vibrant and God fearing student that are sound academically, morally and in all dimension.

Fire control equipment in line with the regulation of the fire service department is also provided.

The University Health Centre opens 24hrs with qualified doctors, and nurses and other paramedical support to serve the health needs of our students.
There is adequate provision for electricity, water and telecommunication. The school is fenced and provided with gates manned adequately by the school management-for security of lives and properties.
